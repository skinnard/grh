The Microsoft Suface contest starts May 5, 2015 at 10:00AM Eastern Time (“ET”) and ends on June 30, 2015 at 3:00PM ET (collectively, the “Contest Period”).

The sponsor of this contest is The Source (Bell) Electronics Inc. (“Contest Sponsor”).

No purchase is necessary to enter or to win. Only one (1) entry per email address is permitted. To enter the contest:

  1. Visit online at thesource.ca/contests and follow the contest link to complete the entry form; or visit www.iwantthatsurface.ca.
  2. Click on the “Get Started” button.
  3. Explore features and choose Top 5 favourites.
  4. Fill out Registration form.
  5. Opt-in to emails from The Source for an extra ballot.
  6. Additional ballots can be acquired by sharing on social media and if friends enter contest through user's link.

If entering the contest using your mobile phone: a) Visit online at www.iwantthatsurface.ca, b) Fill out the contest entry form. 

Entries become the property of theContest Sponsor and will not be returned.

Ten (10) grand prize winners will be selected at random from among all eligible entries received during the Contest Period.  The Grand Prize Draw will take place at The Source Store Support Centre located at 279 Bayview Drive, in Barrie, ON, on the following dates:

  1. May 12, 2015 at 11:00 AM – One (1) winner will be selected
  2. May 19, 2015 at 11:00 AM – One (1) winner will be selected
  3. May 26, 2015 at 11:00 AM – One (1) winner will be selected

Entrants selected to be awarded a prize must successfully answer a skill-testing question. If a selected entrant(s) cannot be reached within 72 hours of the random draw, declines the prize, incorrectly answers the skill-testing question, or if a selected entrant fails to meet any of the contest conditions, his/her entry will be voided and another selected entrant will be randomly chosen from the remaining valid entries. In order to be awarded a prize, each selected entrant must correctly answer, without mechanical or other assistance, a time-limited mathematical skill-testing question to be administered by telephone, email or mail at a mutually convenient time. Selected entrants will be contacted by the Contest Sponsor by telephone or email. The odds of winning depend upon the number of eligible entries received. Prize(s) must be accepted as awarded and are not transferable, exchangeable, or convertible to cash except in the sole discretion of the Contest Sponsor. If, as a result of an error relating to the entry process, drawing, or any other aspect of the contest, there are more potential winners of prizes selected than contemplated in these rules, there will be a random draw amongst all such eligible prize claimants after the contest closing date to award the correct number of prizes. A selected entrant will be drawn from all eligible entries which were submitted at the time of each draw to be awarded a Prize (draw dates listed above). It is to the Contestant’s advantage to submit a proper entry early so as to be entered into multiple draws (dates listed above). There is a limit of one prize, grand prize or secondary prize per person per household during the Contest Period. If a selected entrant’s name is selected for the May 12 draw, their entry is null and void for all remaining draws (during the Contest Period).
