Meet the team at Mind Your Manners

I’m a Certified Professional Dog Trainer (CPDT-KA) and co-owner of Follow the Leader Inc Dog Training. I’m also a certified Canine Good Neighbour (CGN) evaluator, a member of the Pet Professional Guild (PPG) and and the Canadian Kennel Club (CKC). I’ve been working with dogs for the last 8 years both as a trainer and a dog walker and I love every minute of it! If you’d like to watch me in action, you can see me as a regular guest on CityLine as the resident dog trainer as well as many other media appearances from SunTV to Canadian Press. I have two crazy little Maltese named Quincy and Pearl and am a proud mom and wife on top of it all!

I've been working with animals in one form or another for as long as I can remember. I previously owned and operated my own dog boarding facility in Horseshoe Valley called Pet Paradise.Since moving to Toronto I've obtained Certificates from George Brown College in Animal Care, Wildlife Rehabilitation, and Animal Enforcement. I also have a diploma in Wildlife Conservation. I've owned and loved dogs all my life and am so happy to be able to work everyday at a job I love with the most amazing bunch of dogs :)
