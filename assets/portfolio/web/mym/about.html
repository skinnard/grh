At Mind Your Manners Dog Walking, we treat your dog like it's one of our own. Fully licensed and insured, you can rest easy knowing your dog is getting the best care possible!


Mind Your Manners is owned and operated by Danielle Hodges, who is a Certified Professional Dog Trainer (CPDT). That means that we teach our walkers about dog body language, safe handling, and signs of stress and aggression. We don’t subscribe to any “old school” beliefs about dogs. We don’t allow choke chains, prong collars, or any other device or technique that will intimidate your dogs while they’re out with us. We believe strongly in dog handling that is reward-based and rooted firmly in behavioural science.
