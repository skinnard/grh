Accessibility Services

Eastlink is committed to meeting our customers’ needs. Call us at 1-888-345-1111, dial *611 on your wireless phone, or use the Live Chat at the tab on the right side of every page on our website to speak to an Eastlink representative about our services available for our customers with accessibility needs. You can also ask any Eastlink representative to receive your bill in an alternative format.

Or, visit the relevant section below for more details on available services.

Accessibility Services information for Wireline Phone

Wireline Phone

Eastlink IP Relay Service

Eastlink IP Relay is a relay service for deaf, hard of hearing or speech impaired persons with Eastlink local phone or wireless service. The service allows you to place and receive text-based relay calls from your personal computer and/or data-enabled mobile wireless device. For more information, please click here.

Eastlink IP Relay Service

The Message Relay Service enables customers with hearing or speech accessibility needs, and who use a Teletypewriter (TTY), to communicate with any other person via the telephone. For more information, please click here.

Voice Carry Over

Voice Carry Over allows customers with hearing accessibility needs but who can speak without difficulty to receive text relay of the telephone conversation while still speaking on your own behalf. For more information, please click here.

Hearing Carry Over

Hearing Carry Over allows you to hear what the other party is saying while relaying your messages via text communications. For more information, please click here.
