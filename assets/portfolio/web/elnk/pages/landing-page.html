Current customer?

You’ll need the following information when you contact us to book your move:

  • Current Eastlink phone number or account number so we can look up your account
  • Your contact information during your move (i.e. the best way to reach you)
  • Full mailing and civic address of where you’re moving from and to, including postal codes, and preferred disconnection and connection dates
  • If you’re moving to an existing residence: how many rooms would you like to have TV and phone installed in?
  • If you’re building a new home: is your home wired for TV and phone services?

You can contact us by phone at 1-888-345-1111. Use live chat, or visit your nearest Eastlink retail location.
